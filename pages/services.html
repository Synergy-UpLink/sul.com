What We Do

Services built around
outcomes, not deliverables

We work across four broad areas — and most engagements draw from several at once. Every solution is scoped to your specific situation, not a pre-packaged offering.

01
Service Area

Digital Infrastructure

The foundation everything else runs on. When your infrastructure is solid, your team stops fighting technology and starts using it.

Web Presence

Website design, development, and hosting. From simple brochure sites to complex platforms with member portals, donation systems, or e-commerce.

Domains & Email

Professional domain setup, email hosting, DNS management, and deliverability — so your organization looks and functions like it means business.

Cloud & Hosting

Reliable hosting on AWS, GCP, or managed platforms. We right-size infrastructure to your actual needs and budget, not to maximize complexity.

APIs & Integrations

Connecting your tools so data flows automatically. No more copy-pasting between systems, no more spreadsheets as the source of truth.

Security & Reliability

Backups, access controls, SSL, monitoring, and incident response planning. Small organizations are targets too — we help you stay protected.

Custom Solutions

When off-the-shelf doesn't fit, we build what you need — databases, internal tools, automation scripts, and purpose-built software.

02
Service Area

Communication & Content

Getting your message out clearly and consistently — in writing, audio, video, or whatever format reaches your audience best.

Copywriting & Editing

Website copy, grant language, annual reports, newsletters, and anything else that needs to be clear, compelling, and true to your voice.

Podcast Production

Full-service podcast setup and production: equipment selection, recording workflow, editing, publishing, and distribution strategy.

Newsletter Systems

Email platform setup, list management, template design, and ongoing editorial support for consistent, professional outreach.

Video & Multimedia

Recording setups, workflow design, and light editing support for organizations adding video to their communications toolkit.

Content Strategy

Editorial calendars, channel strategy, and a realistic content plan that fits your team's actual capacity — not an aspirational one.

Public-Facing Materials

Decks, one-pagers, event programs, and presentation support for speaking engagements, funders, and community outreach.

03
Service Area

Operational Technology

The tools your team uses every day — and the support to make sure they actually use them well.

CRM & Constituent Management

Selecting, implementing, and configuring the right CRM for your organization. Data migration, cleanup, and staff onboarding included.

Project & Team Tools

Setting up and training your team on project management platforms, internal wikis, and collaboration tools that actually get adopted.

Payment & Finance Systems

Donation platforms, payment processors, invoicing systems, and the integrations that keep your financial data in one place.

Workflow Automation

Identifying and building automations that save your team hours each week — from onboarding sequences to reporting pipelines.

Staff Training

Hands-on training sessions, documentation, and recorded walkthroughs so your team feels confident — and keeps feeling that way.

Tech Support & Maintenance

Ongoing support for organizations that need someone to call when something breaks — or just when they have a question.

04
Service Area

Strategic Consulting

Senior-level perspective for organizations making important decisions about technology — without the enterprise price tag.

Technology Audits

A clear-eyed assessment of your current tech stack: what's working, what's not, what's costing you more than it should.

Roadmapping

A phased technology plan aligned to your organizational goals — so investments happen in the right order, for the right reasons.

Vendor Selection

Unbiased guidance on software and service providers. We help you evaluate options, ask the right questions, and avoid regrettable contracts.

Data Strategy

How you collect, store, use, and protect data — from constituent records to program outcomes to financial reporting.

Fractional Tech Leadership

Part-time CTO or technology director support for organizations that need strategic guidance without a full-time hire.

Grant & Budget Support

Technology sections of grant applications, budget justifications, and cost-benefit analysis for funders and boards.
